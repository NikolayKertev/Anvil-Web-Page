Info            
  This sheet focuses on weapon stats, shots-to-kill, effective HP, and more.
Only Tier 1 weapons are included, as damage values remain consistent across tiers.
Time-to-kill (TTK) isn’t included, since it varies with reload-speed modifiers and fire-rate scaling that would require frame-by-frame timing tests for each weapon.
However, TTK analysis and data-driven weapon rankings are planned for future updates.
The goal is to provide reliable baseline data for comparing weapon performance and understanding real damage behavior.		             Gunstats - ingame testing/screenshots several wikis (some of the gun stats had to be gathered from the wiki and could be outdated most whe where able to confirm.  
               
               
              EHP - Reverse engineerd dmg to players hp&shield, threw rigerous ingame testing and watching serverslam footage.  
               
              STK - After figuring out DMG per hit you can calculate the stk and go from there.
